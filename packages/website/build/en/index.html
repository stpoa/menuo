About us

Gain an advantage over other restaurants

Many restaurant customers are dissatisfied with the long wait times for:

  • bringing menu
  • taking the first order
  • trying to order next dishes
  • inform you about your willingness to pay
  • making the payment

Do you want to prevent this?

The idea is simple - let the customer do all these activities on his smartphone!

How does it work?

Quick and easy...

  • place a small stand with brief instructions and a QR code to scan on each table
  • the customer scans the QR code and then opens the menu of your restaurant - without installing, logging in or providing any data, then selects products and makes an order
  • the waiter assigned to the table receives the order notification on his smartphone
  • the customer may at any time use the application to call a waiter for any purpose or inform them about the willingness to pay

DEMO

After scanning the QR code by the customer...

His smartphone will open an application containing your menu, which does not require installation, logging in or even providing any data. This allows him to do the following:

  • browsing Your menu
  • ordering at any time
  • preview list of already ordered products
  • calling the waiter for various purposes (payment - card/ cash, other)

Don't be afraid - you can click here and test it!

And the waiters...

Waiters who will be assigned to the table will receive an order notification with details of their order in their smartphone. What more does the application allow?

  • choice of tables they will serve
  • order notifications
  • notifications of a customer's willingness to pay or other customer needs
  • order acceptance, which the customer will see as a notification on his smartphone
  • the indication of the meals already served
  • setting the priority (sqquence) of dishes
  • deleting completed orders
