About us

Gain a competitive advantage over other restaurants

Many restaurant customers are unsatisfied when they have to wait (sometimes for a long time) for:

  • the menu to be brought to the table
  • taking the first order
  • the possibility of ordering the next dishes
  • informing you about their willingness to pay
  • making the payment



Do you want to prevent these from happening?

The idea is quick and simple - all you need is our APP and your customers will be able to take actions mentioned above simply with their smartphone!

How does it work?

The steps are quick & easy...

  • on every table in your restaurant place a small MENUO sticker (or a stand) with brief instructions, an NFC chip, and a QR code
  • the customer brings the phone closer to a stand or simply scans the QR code, and once it’s done our APP opens your restaurant’s menu in front of your client - without having to install anything, logging in, or providing any data. After the products are selected, an order is filed
  • the waiter assigned to the table receives on his device a notification about a new order
  • the customer at any time may use MENUO APP to call a waiter for any purpose (such as making additional order or informing about his willingness to pay)

Sounds interesting?

Get in touch with us. Due to the current situation the implementation and first month of use are free of charge!

Freqeuntly Ask Questions

How does it work from the customer's side?

After placing the MENUO stand or sticker with a QR code and an NFC chip on each table of your restaurant, customers can use them to launch an APP on their smartphones. It does not require installation or providing any personal data. Using the MENUO APP, customers can place an order, inform the waiter about the willingness to pay or any other needs.

How does it work from the restaurant's side?

Each waiter receives a login and a password. After logging in, he chooses the tables from which he will receive notifications. Of course, at any time he can add and delete each of the selected tables. One can also use a model in which many waiters use one common tablet. The APP works on smartphones, tablets, and computers.

What does the implementation of MENUO in a restaurant look like?

First, you fill in our contact form. After working out the details, within 2-4 working days, we send instructions along with logins and passwords for waiters, as well as stickers for all tables. From now on, your clients can enjoy innovative and alternative customer service.

Can we start using MENUO fully remotely?

Of course, it is possible to implement MENUO anywhere in the world remotely. For further information, use our contact form.

What are the costs of implementing and using MENUO?

We understand the current situation of restaurants, so implementation and the first month of using MENUO are entirely free of charge. You can also use a read-only option (without an APP for waiters and the possibility to place orders) - which works as a digital menu. Contact us in regard to the monthly costs of using MENUO in your country.

For how long do I have to sign the contract and what is the period of notice?

After the first month of using MENUO, the contract is signed for an indefinite period but if you do not feel that our APP gives you the value you need - you can terminate the contract with a 1-day notice period.

What about the waiters?

A waiter who serves the selected table will receive a detailed order notification on their smartphone. What else does MENUO enable?

  • selecting a table that a waiter will serve
  • notifications about the order, a customer's willingness to pay or other needs
  • accepting the order, which the customers will see as a notification on their smartphones
  • order acceptance, which the customer will see as a notification on his smartphone
  • unchecking the meals already served
  • setting the priority (sequence) of dishes
  • deleting completed orders
