About us

Gain an advantage over other restaurants

Many restaurant customers are dissatisfied with the long wait times for:

  • bringing menu
  • taking the first order
  • trying to order next dishes
  • inform You about Your willingness to pay
  • making the payment

Do You want to prevent this?

The idea is simple - let the customer do all these activities on his smartphone!

How does it work?

Quick and easy...

  • place a small MENUO stand with brief instructions, NFC chip and a QR code to scan on each table
  • the customer holds the phone close to a stand or scans the QR code and then opens the menu of Your restaurant without installing, logging in or providing any data - then selects products and makes an order
  • the waiter assigned to the table receives the order notification on his smartphone
  • the customer may at any time use the application to call a waiter for any purpose or inform them about the willingness to pay

Sounds interesting?

Freqeuntly Ask Questions

How does it work from the customer side?

After placing a MENUO stand or sticker with a QR code and NFC chip on each table of Your restaurant, the customer can use them to launch applications on their smartphone. It does not require installation or providing personal data. Then using MENUO, the customer can place an order, inform the waiter about the willingness to pay and any other needs.

How does it work from the restaurant side?

Each waiter receives his login and password. After logging in, he chooses the tables which he will receive notifications from. Of course at any time he can add and delete each of the selected tables. The application works on both smartphone, tablet and computer.

What does MENUO implementation in a restaurant look like?

Firstly You send hi@menuo.app e-mail with Your menu, table ID's and number of the waiters. Then within 2-4 business days, we send instructions along with logins and passwords for waiters, as well as stands or stickers for all tables. From now on, You can enjoy innovative, alternative customer service.

Can we start using MENUO in our restaurant fully remotely?

Of course it is possible to implement MENUO anywhere in the world remotely. For details, contact us by e-mail, phone or messenger.

What are the costs of implementing and using MENUO?

We understand the current situation of the restaurant so implementation and the first 3 months of use are completely free. Contact us regarding MENUO monthly costs in Your country.

How long do I have to sign the contract for and what is the notice period?

After a period of 3 months of use the contract is signed for an indefinite period but if You do not feel that our application gives You the right value - You can terminate the contract with a 1-day notice period. There are no hidden hooks.

And the waiters...

Waiters who serve the selected table will receive an order notification with details of their order in their smartphone. What more does the application allow?

  • choice of tables they will serve
  • order notifications
  • notifications of a customer's willingness to pay or other customer needs
  • order acceptance, which the customer will see as a notification on his smartphone
  • the indication of the meals already served
  • setting the priority (sqquence) of dishes
  • deleting completed orders
